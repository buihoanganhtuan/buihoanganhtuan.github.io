About me

Hello, nice to meet you. I am Bui Hoang Anh Tuan, a third-year Ph.D. student at the 会津大学 (University of Aizu), Japan.
Five years ago, I graduated from my university and left my home country of Vietnam to come to Japan and embarked on a new journey, a new chapter in my life.
I've had many firsts during these five years, and found myself changed in more than one ways. I've come to understand what it really means to grow up. More importantly, I found my horizon broadened greatly in both personal and professional senses.

I will graduate in this September of 2021, and am currently looking for a job. I really hope that I can continue my life in Japan, working hard to continue improving myself and, at the same time, enjoy the wonderful nature and culture the country has to offer.
If you are interested, don't hesitate to drop me a chat on Skype (my social handles at the bottom of this website). If everything works out, then great. If it doesn't, we can still be good friends :)
